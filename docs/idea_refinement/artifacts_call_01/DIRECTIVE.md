## Objectives
1. Reduce total workflow wall-clock time by minimizing subprocess cold-start overhead and redundant I/O. [DECISION]
2. Eliminate duplicate logic and consolidate validation paths. [DECISION]
3. Reduce per-stage context-window pressure through prompt deduplication and delta delivery. [DECISION]
4. Remove synchronous event-loop blockers and throttle unnecessary render work. [DECISION]
5. Maintain output contract integrity across all artifacts. [DECISION]

## Policy: OPTIMIZATION
The optimization policy pursues incremental, safe changes with measurable latency reductions. It favors low-risk micro-optimizations (debounced I/O, memoized paths, regex compilation) that preserve existing architecture. This policy is secondary for this run. [DECISION]

## Policy: CREATIVITY/EXPLORATION
The creativity/exploration policy prioritizes structural experiments: single-session runner reuse, delta-prompting after loop 1, append-only manifest streams, and scoped incremental snapshots. This policy accepts higher implementation uncertainty in exchange for order-of-magnitude gains. This is the primary policy. [DECISION]

Selected Policy: CREATIVITY/EXPLORATION
